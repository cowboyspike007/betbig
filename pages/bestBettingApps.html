Indian betting sites payment method

An increasing number of bookies have recognised just how big betting has become in India. As a result, payment methods suiting Indian customers are likely to be found, with Rupees also being a widely accepted currency. So, when it comes to depositing and withdrawing funds, the following are the most common options, allowing punters to place bets with ease:

Cards & Indian-Specific Payment Methods

Google Pay : Google Pay has made our lives easier in so many ways and when it comes to looking at it as a betting site payment method, google pay has started gaining popularity. Google pay casinos are available in abundance as this payment option is not only accessible via UPI & Net banking but also supports secure deposits & withdrawals at Indian bookmakers. Google pay works great on both Android as well as iOS devices and the best part is the fee is minimal.

Visa : One of the most renowned payment methods globally, the Visa card is accepted by top betting sites due to its secure & trusted nature. A Visa card is a good option when it comes to making a deposit as punters can easily claim the welcome offer which is not the case with Neteller or Skrill. It offers immediate processing time to prevent any kind of waiting while wagering. While the Visa card comes with the benefit of funding 3rd party e-wallet and no additional fee is charged, there are certain minor complications as banks tend to say no to wagering & gaming transactions.

PayPal : PayPal is quite renowned among the Indian audiences pertaining to top betting website transfers. It is safe to deposit & withdraw via PayPal. The only con is that PayPal casinos charge high transaction fees but other than that it is a reliable way to play online casino games.

Bitcoin : Some top betting sites in India accept payment via Bitcoin. The transactions via Bitcoin casinos are swift and require no additional fee. Players who are afraid of identity theft can relax because with Bitcoin casinos you will not have to link your bank account. Wagering with Bitcoin can come with its own set of cons which include extra effort to withdraw the money in your traditional currency and market fickleness. The value of Bitcoin keeps on changing as per the market conditions.

PhonePe : PhonePe is another widely established India wallet and UPI deployed app, similar to Paytm. Many top betting sites in India permit the use of PhonePe wallet for deposits & withdrawals and many punters prefer this popular app because it charges no processing fees and provides reliable transfer of funds.

MuchBetter : Set in motion in 2017, MuchBetter is an app-based payment method and can be considered a great alternative to traditional banking options, especially in the online gambling space. MuchBetter Casinos has low deposit & withdrawal fees and they also support transactions in Indian rupees. There are various funding options available. However, the only con with this globally known app is the lack of a Mastercard option in India.

UPI : Available and compatible with only Indian punters, the Unified Payment Interface(UPI) is a concurrent payment system which is regulated by the Indian government. What needs special attention is the fact that UPI is acknowledged and accepted by the Indian bookies as a relevant method for deposit & withdrawal. One drawback with UPI casinos is the low withdrawal limit, other than this payment method is reliable, safe & easy to use.

Skrill : Skrill, An e-wallet that gained traction, particularly because of being an online betting deposit method. This payment method is best for Indian players who do not want to share bank details. Skrill accepts INR transfers and follows a two-factor authorisation method. However, it has been noticed that many bookies will prohibit Skrill payments from accessing bonuses. Make sure you are going through the terms and conditions. Also, Skrill charges a fee of 2.50% on your casino deposit.
